Our Story

In 1975, Mahesh Shah started Mahajan Kirana as a small neighbourhood store built on hard work, honest pricing, and genuine customer care.

"Every Customer is Family" is the founding belief that still guides the store.

Over time, Harendra Prasad Shah expanded the business, widened the product range, and built strong trust with families across Biratnagar.

Today, Harshit Shah is carrying that legacy forward online, making ordering faster and easier while keeping the same local warmth.
