About Mahajan Kirana Store

Mahajan Kirana Store is a family-run local kirana and grocery store in Biratnagar, Nepal. Since 1975, we have served customers with fair pricing, quality products, and dependable support for daily household needs.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: provide essential groceries, FMCG items, and trusted service to every family in Biratnagar. We focus on value, product freshness, and long-term customer trust.

What We Offer

  • Daily grocery essentials and staples
  • Wide range of FMCG products
  • Retail and wholesale support
  • Direct ordering via WhatsApp, phone, and email
